About

This site was created as part of my first year in BUT MMI (Multimedia and Internet Professions). It responds to a subject entitled "Prehistory of the Future", which consists of projecting oneself into a future era and examining the ways of life of the past, here those of families of the 21st century.

The project explores family relationships through works such as books, films, and songs, analyzing them with the perspective of a future historian. The goal is to understand the tensions, silences, and misunderstandings that prevailed in families of that time, while the world was undergoing significant transformation.

It is a critical reflection combining culture, history, and web creation, linking our current era to an imaginary future. This work allows me to apply the skills acquired in design, development, and analysis.
